Baby Neurons Memorization vs generalization

Tiny data memorizes. More data generalizes.

A high-capacity model can drive train loss near zero on 20 examples while held-out loss stays high. Give it 200 or 2000 examples from the same noisy rule and the train/test gap shrinks. Data closes the gap.
